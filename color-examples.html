tinycss — colour combinations

Every palette below is generated from just two OKLCH hue angles — a primary (the hero: links, buttons) and a secondary (orientation: tags, focus, code, blockquotes). Set them with --hue / --alt-hue and the whole ramp, in light and dark, follows.

Rendered with the real shared-ramp chroma, so what you see is what you get (cool hues gamut-map to a calmer primary). All combinations shown clear WCAG AA. See Colors.md for the full rationale.
